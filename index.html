About me

I'm a 3D artist specializing in crafting high-quality, game-ready assets, with a particular passion for foliage, vegetation, and environmental props. My work revolves around designing optimized, visually captivating assets that blend seamlessly into any game world, optimized for performance.

After finishing my Bachelor's degree in Industrial Design, I pivoted to the gaming industry in 2020. Here, you'll find information about my meticulously crafted asset packs, from detailed foliage packs to versatile low poly collections, each created to provide game developers with the tools they need for dynamic storytelling.
